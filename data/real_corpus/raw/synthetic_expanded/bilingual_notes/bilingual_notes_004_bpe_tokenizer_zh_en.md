# BPE Tokenizer Notes / BPE 分词器说明

Project-authored synthetic educational example for controlled corpus expansion.

## English
A BPE tokenizer learns reusable subword pieces from a training corpus.
It can represent frequent words efficiently while still handling rarer text through smaller units.

## 中文
BPE 分词器会从训练语料中学习可复用的子词片段。
它既能较高效地表示高频词，也能通过更小的片段处理较少见的文本。

## Shared idea / 共同点
The tokenizer is a compression and segmentation layer before model training.
分词器是在模型训练之前进行压缩与切分的一层。

## Summary / 总结
A good tokenizer helps the model consume text in a stable, consistent format.
合适的分词器可以让模型以更稳定、一致的形式接收文本。
